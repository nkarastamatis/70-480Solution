position: static | relative | absolute | fixed
used in conjunction with top, right, bottom, left
static
Normal behavior. The top, right, bottom, and left properties do not apply.
relative
Lay out all elements as though the element were not positioned, and then adjust the element's position.
absolute
Do not leave space for the element. Instead, position it at a specified position relative to its closest positioned ancestor or to the containing block.
fixed
Do not leave space for the element. Instead, position it at a specified position relative to the screen's viewport and doesn't move when scrolled.
